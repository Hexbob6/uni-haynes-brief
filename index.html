What's new?

The best-selling Bike Book by Haynes contains invaluable step-by-step guidance on all aspects of bike care, and this new edition has been thoroughly revised and updated to cover the latest developments.

Packed with over 1,000 colour photographs, this manual is the undisputed market leader in bicycle maintenance and an essential tool for every cyclist.

With the Haynes Bike Book and a little skill, all cyclists can have the confidence to tackle regular maintenance, running repairs and even major overhauls.



New in the 7th Edition:

  • New Drivetrain Chapter integrating all aspects of chains, gears, cranks and bottom brackets.
  • Expanded coverage of disc and hydraulic disc brakes.
  • Full suspension bikes – checking, adjustment and replacement of parts.
  • More in-depth coverage of suspension forks.
  • Headset replacement.
  • Wheel rim replacement.
  • BMX bikes – more coverage of brakes and cranks.
  • Troubleshooting – a guide to common problems with suggested causes and remedies.
  • Specifications – common tyre sizes, tightening torques, bottom bracket and headset standards.
